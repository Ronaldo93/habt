<h1 style="text-align: center;">Habt</h1>

An habit tracker that logs your good (and bad) habits and show how you can improve yourself with your data.


# Why build this?
Habits track good one, however there is also a real problem on the opposite: You couldn't commit on stop doing the bad one, at least consistently even when motivated. How many times have you told yourself that this is the last time you scroll on your phone, only to find yourself scrolling until 3am?
